About Recipe Finder

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Recipe Finder is the final project for the Advanced Python(CIS4930). It is a multiuser, web application that uses the Edamam API for recipes and nutrition information. With Recipe Finder users can perform personalized searches for the ingredients they have available and they are shown a list of all possible recipies with these ingredients.
